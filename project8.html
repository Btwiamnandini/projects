Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Strawberries 1 cup
Whipping Cream 1/2 cup
Sugar 2 tbsp
Vanilla Essence 1 tsp

Steps to Prepare

  1. Wash and slice the strawberries.
  2. Whip the cream until soft peaks form.
  3. Add sugar and vanilla to the cream and whip again.
  4. Layer the strawberries and cream in a glass.
  5. Chill for 30 minutes and enjoy!
